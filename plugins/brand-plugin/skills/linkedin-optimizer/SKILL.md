# LinkedIn Profile Optimizer

**Purpose:** Transform LinkedIn profiles from online resumes into powerful personal branding tools using a proven 8-step framework.

## Core Principle

**LinkedIn profiles should be personal branding tools, not resumes.**

## The 8-Step Framework

### Step 1: Professional Headshot
- Quality requirements (min 400x400px, natural lighting, shoulders up)
- Attire appropriate for industry
- Expression: approachable and confident
- Background: professional, not distracting

### Step 2: Banner Design (1584 x 396 px)
Recommend banner content based on goals:
- Value proposition statement
- Trust indicators from achievements
- Clear call-to-action
- Mobile-optimized safe zones

### Step 3: Headline Optimization (220 chars max)
**Formula:** [What You Do] | [Who You Help] | [Unique Value]

Generate 3-5 variations:
1. Audience outcome focus
2. Achievement-first
3. Methodology emphasis
4. Transformation highlight
5. Mission/vision lead

### Step 4: Featured Section
Recommend 3-5 featured items in priority order:
1. Lead magnets or free resources
2. Product/service pages
3. High-value content pieces
4. Newsletter signup
5. Booking/consultation links

### Step 5: About Section (2,600 chars max)
**Structure:**
- Opening Hook (1-2 sentences)
- Journey Narrative (2-3 sentences)
- Challenges & Transformation (2-3 sentences)
- Current Mission (2-3 sentences)
- Specific Results (2-4 sentences)
- Call-to-Action (1-2 sentences)

### Step 6: Experience Section
- Results-focused bullet points (not task descriptions)
- Quantifiable achievements using user's data
- Keyword integration for search
- Company page guidance if they have a business

### Step 7: Content Strategy
- Posting cadence (3-5x/week recommended)
- Content topics aligned with expertise
- Format variety (text, carousel, video, article)
- 10-15 topic ideas based on their niche
- Engagement tactics

### Step 8: Recommendations Plan
- Target list (former clients, colleagues, partners)
- Customized outreach template
- Focus areas for each recommendation
- Strategy for building proof over time
