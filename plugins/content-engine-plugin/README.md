# Content Engine Plugin

Turn one idea into dozens of content pieces — extract ideas, repurpose across platforms, plan your calendar, and define your content pillars.

## Skills

| Skill | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **content-extractor** | Extract platform-specific content ideas from long-form content into detailed briefs |
| **content-repurposer** | Turn 1 piece into YouTube outline + newsletter + thread + LinkedIn + 5 titles |
| **content-calendar** | 4-week calendar with 1 main piece + 3 social posts + 1 note per week |
| **content-pillars** | Define 3-5 strategic content pillars with 10 starter topics each |

## Context Files

All skills read these files if they exist:
- **USER.md** — who you are
- **BUSINESS.md** — your business and positioning
- **ICP.md** — your ideal customer
- Any installed voice/brand voice skill
